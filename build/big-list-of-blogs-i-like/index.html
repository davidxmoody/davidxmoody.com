Big list of blogs I like

Oct 6, 2014

I don't have time to write a proper post right now so here is a big list of some of my favourite blogs. I use Feedly as my RSS reader and save longer articles to Pocket for later reading.

Programming

  • Smashing Magazine: Lots of in-depth front end web design articles
  • CSS-Tricks: As the name suggests, lots of great CSS tricks
  • The Daily WTF: Very funny stories about how software design can go horribly wrong
  • Coding Horror: Great blog about programming and software design, its advice helped shape this blog
  • Code inComplete: Small blog about JavaScript games and other programming stuff
  • Neovim: A Vim re-write attempt, looks very promising

Tech

  • Ars Technica: My favourite "general technology" blog, lots of in-depth articles too
  • Hacker News: Great startup and tech articles (the regular feed is a bit spammy so here is a link to an RSS feed for just the best articles)
  • Narrative Blog: Blog for the Narrative Clip camera but also has some other great self-tracking articles
  • Paul Graham's Blog: Been around for ages, archives are great and still has some good new posts on startups and things

Other

  • James Clear: Plenty of great advice on habit forming
  • Study Hacks: Advice on deep work and how to have a satisfying career (I can also recommend his book)
  • Seth Godin's Blog: Frequent short but well written posts on marketing
  • You Are Not So Smart: Some brilliantly written articles on psychological biases (check the archives)
  • The Ancient Wisdom Project: The author is in the process of choosing one of the major religions to follow for a month and writing about what he learns
  • Less Wrong: A community blog about the art of human rationality
  • Minding our way: Some excellent posts on rationality and mental models
  • Dr. Bunsen: Occasional very interesting articles about statistics, Vim and other things
  • David R. MacIver: Just a blog by a guy who's interested in similar things to me
  • xkcd: The best web comic ever
  • Saturday Morning Breakfast Cereal: The second best web comic ever
